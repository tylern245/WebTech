About Me/Bio

Hello there! My name is Tyler Nguyen and I am 22-year-old senior at the University of Texas at San Antonio. I am currently pursuing a B.A. in computer science! I was born in St. Louis, Missouri on December 1, 1999. I lived in a household both of my parents, who were immigrants from Vietnam, and my sister, and we were a lower-middle class family. After about five years, my parents said the area was too cold and it was bad for my sister's asthma, so we moved to Houston, Texas where the weather we generally a lot warmer (or rather hot). Houston is where I grew up and lived most of my life.

I've always loved being around computers and technology in general, but I never thought about growing up to be in the technology field until my senior year of high school. Growing up, I went through some phases, including piano, soccer, basketball, and high school band. But those were activities I chose because of my parents, my friends, or as a requirement for school. I can't say I have a real passion for any of those things.on and my career path. Coming from a person who didn't grow up with a lot of money, the salary is also a good motivator.
